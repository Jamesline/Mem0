Elon Musk is the CEO of Tesla, an electric car maker, as well as the founder of several other companies including SpaceX and the Boring Company. He is known for his innovative ideas and contributions to the fields of electric vehicles, space exploration, and tunneling. Musk has also pledged a significant portion of his shares in Tesla as collateral for personal loans. He has a background in coding and has sold his first game, Blastar, as a child. Musk has expressed concerns about population collapse and has ten children with three women.
